Letter for my Parents

MERRY CHRISTMAS MOMMY AND DADDY!!!


Thank so much for for everything! Thank you for taking care of me always, for understanding me whenever I feel upset, and for your never ending support.

Thank you for making every Christmas memorable and happy from day one.Thank you for loving me and providing for me every day. Thank you for giving me a great education by supporting me through college. Thank you for making Christmas special for me no matter how old I get. Thank you for leading me to grow deeper in the Lord, and trusting Him in any circumstances. Thank you for always making me happy!


Having you as parents is the greatest gift anyone could receive for Christmas.


I love you mommy and daddy!!! Merry Christmas!


From your loving daughter,

Alyza Claire
