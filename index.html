The Christian Meaning Behind Ancient Traditions

Candles

John 8

Candles are a picture that Christ is the Light of the world.

Holly

Matthew 27:29

Holly speaks of the thorns in His crown.

Red

Red is a color of Christmas that speaks of Christ's blood and death.

Gifts

Matthew 2

Gifts are a reminder of the gifts of the Magi to baby Jesus.

Mistletoe

Romans 5:1

It was under mistletoe that old enmities and broken friendship were restored. Christ was the One who took away the enmity and gave us peace with God.

Bells

Bells are associated with ringing out news. Christ is the good news, the best news of all.
